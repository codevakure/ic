# Ranger Account Setup & Okta Configuration Guide (Internal Team QA)

Follow this step-by-step guide to set up your Ranger account with Okta authentication. The complete process includes receiving your invitation, configuring your credentials, setting up Okta authentication, and accessing the home screen.

---

## Step 1: Get Your Invitation

You will receive an email invitation from your organization or administrator to join Ranger. This invitation contains a secure link to begin the account setup process.

**What to do:**
- Check your email inbox for the Ranger invitation
- Look for an email from your organization or IT department
- If you don't see it, check your spam/junk folder



---

## Step 2: Enter Your Email ID

Click the activation link in your invitation email to begin the setup process. You'll be taken to the Ranger registration page where you'll need to confirm your email address.

**What to do:**
- Click the activation link in your invitation email
- Enter your email address when prompted
- Ensure it matches the email where you received the invitation

![Email Login](/images/emaillogin.png)

---

## Step 3: Enter Temporary Password

You'll be provided with a temporary password to access your account for the first time. This password is usually included in your invitation email or provided separately by your administrator.

**What to do:**
- Enter the temporary password provided by your administrator
- Keep this password secure until you change it
- Make note of any password requirements for the next step

![Password Login](/images/passwordlogin.png)

---

## Step 4: Change Your Password

For security purposes, you must change your temporary password to a permanent one that meets your organization's password requirements.

**What to do:**
- Create a strong, unique password
- Follow your organization's password policy requirements
- Confirm your new password by entering it twice
- Store your password securely

![Change Password Login](/images/changepasswordlogin.png)

---

## Step 5: Okta Connection Initialization

After changing your password, you'll be redirected to connect with your organization's Okta system. This establishes the secure connection between Ranger and your company's authentication system.

**What to do:**
- You'll see a "Connecting to" screen with a settings/gear icon
- The message "Sign in with your account to access Ranger Enterprise Chat" will be displayed
- Click on the "Setup" button to proceed with the Okta configuration
- This will initiate the connection to your organization's Okta system

![Setup Okta](/images/setupokta.png)

---

## Step 6: Setup Okta Verify App

You'll now set up the Okta Verify app for multi-factor authentication. This app will be used to authenticate your login attempts.

**What to do:**
- You'll see the "Set up Okta Verify" screen with your username displayed
- **If you see a browser prompt:** Click "Open Okta Verify" to use an existing installation
- **If you don't have Okta Verify installed:**
  - Click "Download here" to download the Okta Verify app on your device
  - Install the app on your smartphone or computer
- **For mobile setup:** Click "set up Okta Verify on a mobile device"
- You will get a QR code to scan - scan this QR code with your mobile device using the Okta Verify app
- Once the app is installed, follow the prompts to add your account to the Okta Verify app
- Complete the setup process and test the authentication

![Setup Okta Mobile](/images/setupoktamobile.png)

### QR Code Scanning

When you choose mobile setup, you'll see a QR code screen:

![QR Scan Okta](/images/qrscanokta.png)

---

## Step 7: Setup Additional Security Methods

After setting up Okta Verify, you'll be directed to configure additional security methods for backup authentication. You'll see the "Set up security methods" screen where you can choose between email and phone verification methods.

**What to do:**
- You'll see the security methods setup screen with your username displayed
- Under "Required now" you'll see Okta Verify (which you just completed)
- Choose your preferred backup authentication method:
  - **Email verification:** Configure email-based authentication for backup access
  - **Phone/SMS verification:** Set up phone number for SMS or voice call verification
- Click "Set up" for your chosen method

![Email Phone Setup](/images/emailphonesetup.png)

---

## Step 8: Phone/SMS Verification Setup

If you select phone verification, you'll be redirected to the phone setup page where you can choose between SMS or voice call verification.

**What to do:**
- Choose between "SMS" or "Voice call" options
- Select your country/region from the dropdown
- Enter your phone number
- Click "Receive a code via SMS" (if you chose SMS option)

![SMS Setup](/images/sms.png)

### Enter Verification Code

After requesting the SMS code, you'll receive a verification code on your phone:

**What to do:**
- Check your phone for the SMS verification code
- Enter the code in the "Enter Code" field
- Click "Verify" to complete the phone verification

![Enter Code](/images/entercode.png)

---

## Step 9: Welcome to Ranger - Home Screen

Congratulations! You've successfully completed the account setup process. After verifying your phone number, you'll be redirected to the Ranger home screen where you can begin using the application.

**What you'll see:**
- The main Ranger dashboard with a personalized greeting
- Navigation menu and available features on the left sidebar
- Your account information in the top right corner
- Quick action buttons for common tasks like "Write a first draft", "Get advice", "Learn something new", etc.
- Message input area at the bottom to start chatting with Ranger

![Ranger Home Screen](/images/screen.png)

## Troubleshooting & FAQs

### Didn't receive a Ranger invitation email?
- Check your spam or junk folder
- Confirm with your IT team that your email address is correct
- Request a new invitation from your administrator if needed

### Issues with temporary password?
- Ensure you're entering the password exactly as provided
- Check for any leading/trailing spaces
- Contact your administrator if the password doesn't work

### Problems with Okta setup?
- Ensure you have the correct Okta credentials
- Contact your IT support if you can't access Okta
- Verify you have the Okta Verify app installed on your device

### Okta Verify not working?
- Check your device's internet connection
- Ensure the Okta Verify app is up to date
- Try using your backup authentication method
- Contact IT support if issues persist

### Can't complete security method setup?
- Ensure your phone number is correct for SMS/call methods
- Try a different security method if one isn't working
- Contact your administrator for assistance

### Need help after reaching the home screen?
- Explore the user guide and documentation
- Look for help or tutorial sections within the application
- Contact your organization's support team for training

## Security Best Practices

- **Never share your credentials:** Keep your Okta and Ranger login information private
- **Use strong passwords:** Follow your organization's password policy requirements
- **Keep devices secure:** Protect devices with Okta Verify installed
- **Report issues immediately:** Contact IT support if you suspect unauthorized access
- **Regular updates:** Keep the Okta Verify app updated on your device
