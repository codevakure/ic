# Request through sailpoint

Follow this step-by-step guide to set up your Ranger account with Okta authentication. The complete process includes receiving your invitation, configuring your credentials, setting up Okta authentication, and accessing the home screen.

---

## Step 1: Request Access Through SailPoint

To get access to Ranger, you need to submit a SailPoint request through the TCB Okta dashboard. Follow these steps to request Ranger access:

**What to do:**

1. **Access TCB Okta Dashboard**
   - Go to [tcb.okta.com](https://tcb.okta.com)
   - Log in with your TCB credentials
   

2. **Navigate to SailPoint**
   - From the Okta dashboard, locate and click on SailPoint
   - This will take you to the SailPoint request center
   
   ![Dashboard SailPoint](/images/dashboardsailpoint.png)

3. **Request for Yourself**
   - In SailPoint, go to the Request Centre
   - Select "Request for Yourself"
   
   ![Request for Myself](/images/requestformyself.png)

4. **Search for Ranger**
   - In the search bar, type "Ranger"
   - Select Ranger from the search results
   
   ![Search Ranger Select](/images/searchrangerselect.png)

5. **Choose Your Ranger Access Type**
   
   You will see four available Ranger options. Choose the one that best fits your needs:

   **Available Options:**
   - **Ranger (Admin)** - Administrative access with full system management capabilities
   - **Ranger (Consumer)** - For users who want to query and get responses on banking and Texas Capital Bank website data
   - **Ranger (Economic)** - For users who want access to economic data and financial indicators
   - **Ranger (User)** - Standard user access for general Ranger functionality
   
   ![Ranger Overall](/images/rangeroverall.png)

   ### Ranger (Consumer)
   
   This option is for users who want to query and get responses on banking and Texas Capital Bank website content. It scrapes those websites and provides you with appropriate responses. It also contains training materials for enhanced learning.
   
   ![Ranger Consumer](/images/rangerconsumer.png)
   
   **Key Features:**
   - Website scraping and data retrieval from banking sites including:
     - **Bask Bank** website data and information
     - **Texas Capital Bank** website data and content
   - Comprehensive training materials and educational resources
   - Query and response functionality for banking information and services
   - Real-time access to updated website content and banking information

   ### Ranger (Economic)
   
   This option is for users who want to access comprehensive economic data including 10-Q and 10-K filings for the past 10 years (2015-2025) for major financial institutions and economic indicators.

6. **Select Your Preferred Option**
   - Choose the Ranger access type that matches your needs
   - Click to select your preferred option (e.g., "Ranger (User)" for standard access)
   
   ![Ranger User](/images/rangeruser.png)

7. **Save Selection**
   - Confirm your selection
   - Save the selection to proceed
   
   ![Save Selection](/images/save1selection.png)

8. **Review Request**
   - Click on "Review Request" to verify all details
   - Ensure all information is correct
   
   ![Review Request](/images/reviewrequest.png)

9. **Submit Request**
   - Click "Submit Request" to complete the process
   - Your request will be sent for approval
   
   ![Submit Request](/images/submitrequest.png)

**After Approval:**

Once your SailPoint request is approved, you will receive an email notification. You can then access Ranger through two methods:

### Method 1: Access Through TCB Okta Dashboard

1. **Go to TCB Okta Dashboard**
   - Navigate to [tcb.okta.com](https://tcb.okta.com)
   - Log in with your TCB credentials

2. **Find Ranger Application**
   - You will see Ranger listed in your available applications
   - Look for the Ranger app icon in your dashboard
   
   ![Ranger Dashboard](/images/rangerdashboard.png)

3. **Access Ranger**
   - Click on the Ranger application
   - You will be redirected directly to the Ranger home screen

### Method 2: Direct Access Through Ranger URL

1. **Access Ranger URL**
   - Go directly to [ranger.tcb-internal.net](https://ranger.tcb-internal.net)
   - This will redirect you to the Okta SSO login page

2. **Enter Your Email**
   - Type your email address in the login field
   - Click "Next" to proceed
   
   ![Ranger SSO Login Email](/images/rangerssologinemail.png)

3. **Enter Your Password**
   - Type your password in the password field
   - Click "Verify" to continue
   
   ![Ranger SSO Password](/images/rangerssopassword.png)

4. **Multi-Factor Authentication**
   - Complete the verification process by:
     - Entering the authentication code from your device, OR
     - Accepting the push notification on your registered device
   
   ![Ranger Push Notification](/images/rangerpushnotification.png)

5. **Access Granted**
   - After successful authentication, you will land on the Ranger welcome page

![Ranger Home Screen](/images/screen.png)
