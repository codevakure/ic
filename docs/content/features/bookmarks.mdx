## Bookmarks

The Bookmark feature allows you to save entire chat threads for quick access later, helping you maintain continuity and reduce time spent searching for key information.

### Overview

Bookmarks in Ranger provide a convenient way to save and organize important conversations:

- Mark finalized drafts for future reference
- Highlight important decisions that impact ongoing work
- Save reference threads for ongoing projects
- Create a personal knowledge base of valuable conversations

<Callout type="tip">
  Bookmarks help you build an organized library of your most valuable conversations, making it easy to revisit important information without searching through your chat history.
</Callout>

### How to Add a Bookmark

Adding a bookmark to your current conversation is simple:

1. Click the Bookmark icon located at the top-right of the chat screen
2. Enter a title to clearly identify the bookmark
3. Optionally, add a description to provide additional context or notes
4. Click Save to store the bookmark

<Callout type="info">
  Descriptive titles and clear descriptions make it easier to identify the purpose and content of each bookmark later.
</Callout>

![Bookmark Icon](/images/book.png)

### Accessing Bookmarks

You can quickly access all your saved bookmarks through the left navigation panel:

- Click the Bookmark icon in the left-side navigation panel
- All saved bookmarks are displayed in one organized list
- Select any bookmark to open the saved thread

This centralized access allows you to easily retrieve and reuse entire threads without navigating through your chat history.

<Callout type="tip">
  The Bookmarks section provides a dedicated space for all your important conversations across different topics and projects.
</Callout>

![Bookmark Icon](/images/bookmarkl.png)

### Managing Bookmarks

Once you've created bookmarks, you can organize and maintain them:

**Editing Bookmarks:**
- Update titles and descriptions as projects evolve
- Add additional context to reflect new developments
- Refine categorization for better organization

**Sorting and Filtering:**
- View bookmarks in chronological or alphabetical order
- Filter bookmarks by keywords or topics
- Organize by project or conversation type

**Removing Bookmarks:**
- Delete outdated bookmarks that are no longer relevant
- Remove duplicate information to keep your list streamlined
- Archive completed project bookmarks to maintain a clean workspace

<Callout type="info">
  Regular maintenance of your bookmarks ensures that your reference library remains valuable and easy to navigate.
</Callout>

### Bookmark Best Practices

**Naming Conventions:**
- Use clear, descriptive titles that identify the content
- Include project names or topics for easy identification
- Consider adding dates for time-sensitive information

**When to Bookmark:**
- After reaching an important decision or conclusion
- When creating a template or process you'll reference again
- Upon completing a complex analysis you might need later
- When generating content you'll want to reuse or refine

**Organization Strategies:**
- Create a system for categorizing different types of bookmarks
- Review and clean up your bookmarks periodically
- Use consistent terminology in titles and descriptions

<Callout type="tip">
  Think of bookmarks as your personal knowledge library. The more organized your approach, the more valuable this feature becomes.
</Callout>

### Key Benefits

**Efficiency:**
- Quickly access important chat threads without scrolling through long conversations
- Save time by immediately returning to valuable reference points
- Streamline workflows by maintaining easy access to frequently used information

**Continuity:**
- Maintain continuity across multiple projects or discussions
- Preserve context when returning to a topic after time away
- Ensure consistent information across related conversations

**Collaboration:**
- Easily reference threads for decision-making or project collaboration
- Quickly retrieve important conversations when working with team members
- Maintain a record of key project milestones and decisions

**Knowledge Management:**
- Build a personal library of valuable conversations and insights
- Preserve institutional knowledge for ongoing reference
- Create an organized system of important information

### Summary

The Bookmark feature in Ranger provides a powerful way to maintain organization across your conversations:

- **Save important threads** with descriptive titles and notes
- **Access bookmarks easily** from the left navigation panel
- **Maintain continuity** across projects and discussions
- **Build a knowledge library** of your most valuable conversations

By using bookmarks effectively, you can create a personalized reference system that enhances your productivity and ensures important information is always at your fingertips.
