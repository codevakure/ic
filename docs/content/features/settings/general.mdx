# General Settings

General settings control basic interface preferences and accessibility options to customize your Ranger experience.

**When to Use**: When you want to modify basic interface preferences or accessibility settings.

![General Settings](/images/general.png)

## Theme Settings

### Theme Selection
Choose between Light or Dark mode to optimize your visual experience.

- **Light Mode**: Better visibility in bright settings and professional environments
- **Dark Mode**: Reduces eye strain in low-light environments and during extended sessions

**When to use**: Switch to Dark mode to reduce eye strain in low-light environments or Light mode for better visibility in bright settings.

<Callout type="info">
  The Dark theme is particularly useful for reducing eye strain during night-time work sessions, while Light theme provides better contrast in brightly lit environments.
</Callout>

## Language Settings

### Language Selection
Select your preferred language for the Ranger interface and interactions.

**When to use**: Change when working in multilingual environments or to better understand responses.

This setting affects:
- Interface text and labels
- System messages and notifications
- Response language preferences

## Interface Options

### Markdown Rendering
**Render user messages as markdown**: Enable or disable markdown formatting for your messages.

- **Enable**: For structured content with formatting (headers, lists, links, etc.)
- **Disable**: For plain text only interactions

**When to use**: Enable for structured content with formatting, disable for plain text only.

### Auto-scroll Behavior
**Auto-scroll to latest message on chat open**: Automatically jump to most recent messages when opening a chat.

- **Enable**: Quickly resume recent conversations
- **Disable**: Maintain your place in longer chat histories

**When to use**: Enable to quickly resume recent conversations, disable to maintain your place in longer chats.

### Panel Visibility
**Hide right-most side panel**: Maximize your chat area by hiding the right sidebar.

- **Enable**: More screen space for viewing chat content
- **Disable**: Keep all panels visible for full feature access

**When to use**: Enable when you need more screen space for viewing chat content.

## Archived Chats Management

### Archive Access
**Manage Archived Chats**: Access and restore previously archived conversations.

**When to use**: When you need to reference historical conversations or restore important information.

Features include:
- View all archived conversations
- Restore archived chats to active status
- Permanently delete archived conversations
- Search within archived content

<Callout type="tip">
  Archive chats that are important but not immediately needed to keep your active chat list manageable while preserving access to valuable information.
</Callout>

## Best Practices for General Settings

### Theme Selection
- Use Dark mode during evening or low-light work sessions
- Switch to Light mode in bright office environments or for presentations
- Consider your device's battery life (Dark mode can save battery on OLED screens)

### Language Configuration
- Set to your primary working language for best results
- Consider your team's primary language for shared content
- Update when working on international projects

### Interface Optimization
- Enable markdown for technical documentation or structured content
- Disable auto-scroll when reviewing long conversation histories
- Hide panels when focusing on specific tasks or during presentations

### Archive Management
- Regularly review and archive completed projects
- Restore archived chats when revisiting previous work
- Use archives to maintain organized workspace without losing data

<Callout type="tip">
  Experiment with different combinations of these settings to find what works best for your specific workflow and environment.
</Callout>
