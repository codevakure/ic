# SharePoint & Office 365 Suite

The SharePoint & Office 365 Suite integration provides seamless access to your Microsoft 365 ecosystem directly within Ranger. **This tool requires manual activation** before it can be used.

<Callout type="warning">
  Unlike most other tools, Microsoft 365 integration requires manual selection from the Tools dropdown or Connectors. It will not activate automatically based on your prompt.
</Callout>

## What is SharePoint & Office 365 Suite?

This tool connects Ranger to your Office 365 environment, enabling you to interact with SharePoint, OneDrive, Outlook, and Teams without leaving the chat interface. You can query files, send emails, access documents, and retrieve information from your Microsoft workspace using natural language.

## Available Services

- **SharePoint** - Access and query SharePoint sites, document libraries, and lists
- **OneDrive** - Browse and retrieve files stored in your OneDrive
- **Outlook** - Send emails, read messages, and manage your inbox
- **Teams** - Access messages and chat history

## How to Use SharePoint & Office 365 Suite

**Important:** Microsoft 365 tools must be manually selected before use. They will not activate automatically.

There are two ways to access the SharePoint & Office 365 Suite:

**Method 1: Through Tools Dropdown**
1. Click the **Tools icon** beside the attachment icon in the chat input
2. Select the **ms365** specific service from the dropdown menu (SharePoint, OneDrive, Outlook, or Teams)
3. Ask your question about files, emails, documents, or messages

**Method 2: Through Connectors**
1. Click the **Connectors** option after the Tools symbol
2. You will see **ms365-mcp** in the dropdown

![MCP Connector](/images/mcp_connector.png)

3. Click on **ms365-mcp** - it will authenticate and you will see it as selected

![MCP Connected](/images/mcp_connected.png)

4. Ask your question about files, emails, documents, or messages

<Callout type="info">
  Authentication is required only once per session. After signing in, you can continue asking questions without needing to authenticate again.
</Callout>

## Sending Emails

You can compose and send emails directly through Ranger:

1. Provide the recipient's email address(es)
2. Specify the subject line
3. Describe the message content or provide the full text
4. Ranger will send the email on your behalf

**Example:** "Send an email to liam.parker01@texascapitalbank.com with subject 'Meeting Follow-up' and tell her the report will be ready by Friday."
